1. INTRODUCTION

The program is an adventure survivor game inspired by the movie Maze Runner. In this story a teenage boy wakes up in an old rusty elevator, remembering nothing. Reaching the surface, he finds himself on a big plain filled with grass, called "The Glade". This is a big closed square surrounded by tall stone walls. Food and equipment is delivered regularly by the elevator.

Soon it turns out that our hero's goal is to escape, because the gates on the square-shaped Glade's walls aren't exits but entrances to a so far unsolved labyrinth. What makes it harder to solve the labyrinth is it can only be explored during the day, as in the night it is dominated by biomechanic monsters ("Grievers"). (The routes aren't exactly safe from them in the day either.) The gates connecting the maze and the glade close every evening, and whoever stays in the labyrinth for the night, most likely won't survive until they open in the morning. Another difficulty is that sometimes the layout of the labyrinth changes during the night, closing or opening new entrances.

In the game you'll explore the maze and the creatures in it through various dangerous and exciting situations. We guarantee a form of relaxation in which one can improve their logical, problem solving and concentrating skills.

2. AGE LIMIT

According to PEGI we recommend playing the game from 12 years old.

  • Fear: Pictures and sounds that might be frightening or scary for young children due to the sudden appearance of the enemies, or the way they look.
  • Violence: The game includes non-realistic violence to recognizable animals.

5. MAIN MENU

The first thing you'll see when opening the program is the main menu. You can choose the game mode here. You can change the resolution, audio volume and rebind the controls in the options menu.

6. STORY MODE

In this mode the goal is to explore the maze, and to escape it. Lots of hostile creatures can prevent that, it's up to the player wether he/she fights with them or kills them. There are also friendly NPCs, they can give advice and make trades. The character also needs to eat, drink, find shelter for the night, and defend themselves from hostile creatures to survive. It's more dangerous to wander around at night as enemies will appear more frequently. As time goes by, it'll be harder to survive due to stronger and more enemies.

7. ENDLESS MODE

In this mode you can not escape the maze, the goal is to survive for as long as possible. This mode is only available after playing story mode.

8. GAMEPLAY

You can navigate your character with the W,A,S,D keys and the arrows. Picking up, using objects and fighting can be done with a left or right click.

9. CHARACTERS

The player can choose between a variaty of playable characters. These differ in their numerical stats, such as hit points or speed, their equipment and their abilities. The player gets XP from defeating enemies and progressing in the game. With the right amount of XP, the character can level up, and the player can choose which of their ability to improve. We recommend improving the main abilities (listed below) because that's the most benefitable for each character.

THOMAS

We recommend playing with Thomas first. He can easily handle any kinds of grievers, and is the most likely to survive the night in the labyrinth. He is adventurous, and a bit rebellious. Main abilities: speed, hit points.

TERESA

The only woman in the labyrinth. She has syringes which can cure the sting of the Grievers. She has pretty privilege when it comes to trading. Main abilities: healing, charisma.

ALBY

He's a born leader. He can manipulate any NPCs very easily to make good trades. However he's more likely to get deadly diseases from fighting with enemies. Main abilities: persuasion, charisma.

FRYPAN

The chef. Comes with a frying pan in order to protect himself. Offering his delicious food always helps making better deals with others. Main abilities: cooking, defense.

JEFF

The doctor. He can heal a lot easier due to his knowledge of medicine. He can offer his help to others too. Main abilities: healing, defense.

10. GRIEVERS

By the character leveling up, the hostile creatures also level up. Higher level creatures can cause more damage and can also develop new abilities.

11. PLANTS

MUSHROOM

APPLE

SPINACH

12. SAVE

The game can handle multiple saves, before starting playing the player can choose which one does he/she want to continue in the main menu > “LOAD GAME”. This is advantegous when multiple people are using the same device. The progress is automatically saved when quitting the game, but players can also save and load saves manually by clicking the gear wheel in the upper right corner (except in endless mode).
